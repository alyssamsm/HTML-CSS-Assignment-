Top 3 Things I Need for My Hobby

  • Device: A compatible platform such as:
    1. PC: Windows (minimum specs required)
    2. Mobile: iOS or Android with decent specs (e.g., 8GB+ storage, 4GB+ RAM recommended)
    3. Console: PlayStation 4 or PlayStation 5
  • Stable Internet Connection: A steady internet connection is necessary for downloading game files and maintaining a smooth online experience.
  • miHoYo Account: Create an account on Hoyoverse (formerly miHoYo) to log in and save your progress across platforms.

Steps to Get Started

  1. Download the Game:
    Visit the official Genshin Impact website or your device's app store to download the game. For PC, download the launcher, and for mobile/console, install it directly from the store.
  2. Install and Launch the Game:
    Follow the installation instructions. Launch the game and let it download additional files (this may take some time).
  3. Create or Log in to Your miHoYo Account:
    Sign in with your existing account or create a new one. Choose your server region (e.g., Asia, America, Europe), and you're ready to start your journey in Teyvat!
